SUBJECT:      Children's Health Executive Order


We are providing for your review a revised version of the proposed executive order on children's
health. This version reflects a consensus among White House offices about how best to balance
the goals of the initiative and the concerns about implementation that have been raised among
federal agencies.

In order to identify any remaining agency concerns that should be considered in deciding
whether to move forward with this version of the executive order, CEQ, DPC and NEC will
convene a deputies meeting on Wednesday, April 2, at 4:00 p.m. Please contact Laura Emett at
456-5584 to arrange clearance into the meeting.
