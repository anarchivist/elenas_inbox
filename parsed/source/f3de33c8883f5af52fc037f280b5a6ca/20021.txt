Subject:         Thursday's Schedule




     The Senate will convene Thursday, May 21, at 9:30 a.m., and resume
     consideration of S. 1415, the Tobacco legislation, with the Gregg
     second degree amendment to the Gregg-Leahy amendment striking the
     liability caps pending.

     A roll call vote on a motion to table the Gregg-Leahy amendment may
     occur as early as 11:00 a.m. (No consent agreement has been entered
     regarding this vote.)

     Following disposition of the Gregg-Leahy amendment, Senator Durbin is
     expected to offer an amendment regarding the look-back provisions.

     Upon disposition of the Durbin amendment, the Senate is expected to
     consider the farmer provisions in the modified committee substitute,
     including a vote on the motion to strike the Ford language, followed
     by a vote on the motion to strike the McConnell/Lugar language.

     (No consent agreements have been entered regarding the Tobacco bill
     schedule. )
