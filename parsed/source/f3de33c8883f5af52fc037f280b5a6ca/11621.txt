TEXT:
On Friday, March 12, 1999, the President will travel to Hope, Arkansas, to
dedicate and tour the Clinton Birthplace and Visitors Center.  He will
later proceed to Texarkana, Texas, where he will attend a reception and
dinner for Representative Max Sandlin and tape the radio address.  On
Saturday, the President will return to Arkansas and attend a DNC reception
and state party dinner in Little Rock.  The President will return to the
White House on Sunday.
