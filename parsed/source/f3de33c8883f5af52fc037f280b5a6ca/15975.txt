TEXT:
You are invited (and encouraged) to attend an affirmative action briefing
on June 26, 1997 at 11:00 am.   in the Roosevelt Room.   The briefing will
be conducted by the National Issues Forum,   a non-partisan, network.of
public policy issues sponsored by organizations and institutions within
many communities.   [The forum will be releasing their results publicly at
the National Press Club on June 25, 1997.]   Forum programs are organized
by libraries, colleges, universities and highschools, churches and synagog
ues, literacy and leadership programs, businesses, labor unions, seniors
groups and all kinds of civic associations.   Briefers for this session
will include former Governor William Winter (who is also on the
President's race advisory board), Dr. David Mathews, President of the
Kettering Foundation; John Doble, President of Doble Research; and one or
two the forum moderators.

If you are planning to attend, please let me know bye-mail ASAP. We have
space for about 60 people.  In addition to White House staff, we have
invited staff from the Justice Department Office of Civil Rights, EEOC,
and the Civil Rigths Commission.    Invitations are also be extended to
several of the President's Cabinet.
