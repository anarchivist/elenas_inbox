unanimous consent agreement.
  [SAP sent 7/14: Admin. has serious concerns]

While under consideration, the Senate took the following action on S. 1005:
o Approved (voice vote) Stevens amendment to restore $60 million for the
Nunn-Lugar & related programs, intended to help former Soviet republics dispose


of nuclear & other weapons.
o Senators Stevens and Inouye introduced   an amendment to require a report by
October 1st to specify the costs to NATO   resulting from the admittance of
Poland, Hungary and the Czech Republic.    The report is to specify by account
the U.S. costs for the three new members   for the period FY 1998 through FY
2002.  In addition, the DoD is to report   to the Congress the costs of any new
commitments.

Approved (78-11) motion to invoke cloture on confirmation of Joel KleinD,s
nomination as Assistant Attorney General in the Justice DepartmentD,s
