disapprove the PresidentO,s cancellations of 38 items contained in the

the House must disapprove all cancellations in a particular bill, although
procedures are available to pare down the disapprovals on the House floor.

before the House Budget Committee to discuss long-term budget challenges
at 10:00am in 210 Cannon House Office Building.
