                            STATEMENT OF THE PRESIDENT


I applaud the Senate for taking action today to protect our children from drunk drivers. Today's
vote to make .08 percent BAC the legal limit in all 50 states sends a powerful message: we will
not tolerate the irresponsible acts of those who chose to endanger the lives of all Americans by
drinking and driving. Thanks to the bipartisan leadership of Senators Lautenberg and De Wine,
we can do more to prevent needless alcohol-related crashes in the future and honor the memory
of the thousands of lives cut short by drunk drivers. I challenge the House of Representatives to
follow the Senate's lead and quickly pass this important life-saving measure.
