 CABINET MEMO


 TO:             Michael McCurry

 FROM:   Darby Stott

 DATE:   April 8, 1997


 AGRICULTURE
 No public events.

 COMMERCE
 No public events.

 ONDCP
 Today - Dir. MCCaffrey will attend an international anti-drug abuse
 conference in New York.
 Tomorrow - At 12:30pm, the Director will meet with the Peruvian drug czar.
 He will also meet with the Organization of American States drug czars from
 various countries. The only coverage of this meeting will be at the
 stakeout.

 EDUCATION
 Today - Secs. Riley, Shalala and Slater will read to children on the Mall
 as part of the Magic Bus tour.
 Tomorrow - The Secretary will attend the Shanker funeral.

 ENERGY
 No public events.



 EPA
 Today - The Administrator will testify before the Senate Appropriations
 Committee reo budget.

 FEMA
 Thursday - James Lee Witt will travel to NO and SD to view disaster damage.
 Friday - The Director will view damage in Minnesota.

 HHS
 Today - Secs. Riley, Shalala and Slater will read to children on the Mall
 as part of the Magic Bus tour. Survivors of the Tuskegee experiments are
 asking the government for an apology.
 The Kennedy-Hatch bill on health insurance for children will be active in
 Congress.       ,
 Tomorrow - HHS will officially announce the Ryan White grants.
 The Secretary will hold a press conference with Sen. Mikulski reo
 mammography standards.

 HUD
 Today - The Secretary will hold a press conference in St. Petersburg, FL
 reo housing grants to St. Petersburg for riot recovery. At 3:00pm, the
 Secretary will hold a conference call reo home ownership zones in
 Baltimore, Philadelphia, Buffalo, Cleveland, and Sacramento.
 Tomorrow - The Secretary will testify before the Senate Committee on
 Public Housing.

 INTERIOR
 No public events.

 JUSTICE
 Today - The AG will attend the Canadian Arrival Ceremony.
 Louis Freeh will testify before the Senate Appropriations Committee reo
 child pornography.
 Justice and the FTC will release a joint press release on their guidelines
 for approving corporate mergers.

 OMB
 Today - The Director will address the American Business Council. He will
 also address the Joint Center for Political and Economic Studies.

 TRANSPORTATION
 Today - Secs. Riley, Shalala and Slater will read to children on the Mall
 as part of the Magic Bus tour.

 TREASURY
 Today - The Secretary is in Vietnam. Deputy Secretary Summers will speak
 on the FY 98 budget to multinational development banks.
