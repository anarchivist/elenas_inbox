SUBJECT:   LEGISLATIVE REPORT


The Senate Appropriations Committee today approved the FY97 Disaster/Bosnia
Supplemental by a vote of 16-12.  Floor action is expected next week.  During
committee action on the bill, the Committee defeated a Byrd Amendment to strike
the automatic Continuing Resolution by a vote of 13 to 15 (a party-line vote) .
The Committee also defeated a Hollings amendment to strike the prohibition on


Commerce Department development of sampling for the decennial census by a vote
of 13 to 15.

Deputy Director John Koskinen testified today before the Senate Labor & Human
Resources Committee regarding equal opportunity issues in Federal
construction. Also today, Controller Ed DeSeve testified on the DC
revitalization plan before the House Appropriations DC Subcommittee.
