                                          THE WEEK AHEAD
                                            April 13 - April 19
                                    (All items are subject to change.)


    Monday (4/13): President Clinton and the First Lady will participate in the annual White House
    Easter Egg Roll on the South Lawn. It is the first event to be cybercast from the South Lawn,
    and the largest cybercast in history.

    Tuesday (4/14): President Clinton will travel to Houston, Texas to participate in a national
    conversation on race. Hosted by ESPN, the event will focus on the ways in which sports
    can serve as a model for the President's vision for One America.

    Wednesday (4/15): President Clinton will hold a Tax Day message event to highlight new
    targeted tax cuts (available to Americans filing their 1998 taxes) that will make higher education
    more accessible than ever before. The President will emphasize how these new cuts build on a
    record of fiscally responsible cuts, targeted to create opportunity and maintain our values.

    Thursday (4/16): President Clinton will attend an arrival ceremony in the morning and will
    spend the afternoon with President Frei in bilateral meetings. Later in the afternoon, the
    President will address business and community leaders before attending a State Dinner.

    Friday (4/17): President Clinton will deliver a message to a joint session of the Chilean
    Congress.

    Saturday (4/18): President Clinton will attend the first day sessions of the Summit of the
    Americas. President Clinton will deliver a taped radio address tentatively scheduled for
    food safety.

    Sunday (4/19): President Clinton will attend the second day sessions of the Summit of the
    Americas.
