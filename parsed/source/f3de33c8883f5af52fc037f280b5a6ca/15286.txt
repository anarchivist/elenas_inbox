The House Commerce Telecommunications Subcommittee has scheduled its
second products liability hearing for 4/30.  The hearing will focus on
attorneys fees in class action suits.  The House Judiciary Committee will
have its second hearing on liability legislation on 4/23.  The hearing
will examine legislation (H.R. 911 and H.R. 1167) that grants immunity
from personal civil liability to volunteers working on behalf of
nonprofit organizations and governmental entities.  FYI
