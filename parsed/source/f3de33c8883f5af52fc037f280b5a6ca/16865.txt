 SUBJECT:           LEGISLATIVE REPORT


 Representatives Stenholm (D-TX) and Hulshof (R-MO) introduced a bill today
 to disapprove the PresidentD,s item veto of the farmersD, cooperative
 provision in the Taxpayer Relief Act of 1997. In general, the Congress
 has 30 session days during which to consider the disapproval bill under
 expedited procedures (i.e. late October).  Introduction of the disapproval
 bill does not, itself, impact the cancellation;   the cancellation remains
 in effect unless disapproval legislation is enacted. Even if Congress
 passes the disapproval, the President can veto the disapproval bill
 effectively requiring a 2/3 vote to disapprove the cancellation.
