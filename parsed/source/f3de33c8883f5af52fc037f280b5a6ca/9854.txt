TEXT:
On Thursday, October 29, 1998, the President will travel to the Kennedy
Space Center in Cape Canaveral, Florida, to tour the facilities, meet with
astronauts and their families, and witness the space shuttle launch.
While there,. he will present the Medal of Freedom to former Representative
Dante Fascell. He will then travel to West Palm Beach and attend a
reception for Representative Peter Deutsch and a dinner for the Florida
Democratic party. He will return to the White House late Thursday night.
