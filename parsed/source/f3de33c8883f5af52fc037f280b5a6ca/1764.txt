SUBJECT:           LEGISLATIVE REPORT


Today, Senator Graham (R-TX) and Representative McIntosh (R-ID) introduced
another balanced budget bill.  Their plan has a net tax reduction of $100
billion, saves $100 billion in Medicare, saves $32 billion in Medicaid,
and uses OMB assumptions in some instances. The bill calls for higher
cuts in discretionary spending than any other balanced budget bill,
including Senator DomeniciD,s plan.

strike the exemptions for Tennessee, South Carolina, and Washington.
