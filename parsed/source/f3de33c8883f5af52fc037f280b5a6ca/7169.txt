nominee Dee Lee are scheduled for Wednesday April 22nd.

The Senate reconvened today.     The House was not in session on Monday, but
reconvenes on Tuesday.

Coverdell Education Savings Account Bill, but no rollcall votes occurred.
[POTUS Letter sent, 4/20: Support Moseley-Braun amendment, veto threat on
expanded education IRAs; Riley & Rubin/Riley letter pending: Secretary.

consideration of S. 414, the International Shipping bill. At lOam a
