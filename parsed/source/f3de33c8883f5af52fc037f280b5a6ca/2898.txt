CONGRESS TODAY (6/11):

SENATE
Once again, the Senate adjourned (on a Lott motion 55-44) without further
action on the Supplemental. House and Senate Republican Leadership still
have not corne to agreement on how to proceed. A staff meeting late this
evening with House and Senate bipartisan leadership, Committee staff, and
Administration staff did not produce an agreement.


HOUSE
Passed (voice vote) H.R. 1757 - Foreign Relations Authorization Act,
Fiscal Years 1998-1999. As was ordered in the Rule, H.R. 1758 - European
Security Act - was attached back onto H.R. 1757, and was included in the
billD,s final passage.
         [SAP sent, 6/4: Senior Advisors Veto Threat]
While under consideration, the House took the following action:
o       Passed (225-190) Rohrabacher Amendment which prohibits the
transfer of $95 million in foreign assistance currently earmarked for
Russia in FY 1998 and FY 1999, if Russia transfers an SS-N-22 missile
system to China.
o       Rejected (163-211) Sanford Amendment which limits the total
authorization in the bill for each of fiscal years 1998 and 1999 to the
aggregate amount appropriated in FY 1997.
